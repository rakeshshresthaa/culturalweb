Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our organization, events, and membership. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us and we'll be happy to help!

How do I become a member?

Becoming a member is easy! Simply visit our Membership page and choose the plan that best fits your needs. You can start with our free Basic membership or upgrade to Pro or Team plans for additional benefits.

Are events free for members?

Most of our public events are free for all members. Pro and Team members get access to exclusive workshops and events that may have additional fees, but these are clearly marked when you register.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. Simply log into your account and update your subscription settings.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid memberships. If you're not satisfied with your experience, contact our support team within 30 days of your initial purchase for a full refund.

How can I volunteer at events?

We love volunteers! You can sign up to volunteer through our events page or contact us directly. Volunteers get special perks and early access to event registration.

Is there a minimum age requirement?

Most of our events are open to all ages, but some workshops may have age restrictions due to content or venue requirements. These will be clearly marked in the event details.

How do I stay updated on new events?

We send out a monthly newsletter to all members with upcoming events and news. You can also follow us on social media or check our events page regularly for the latest updates.

Can I bring guests to events?

Yes! Most events allow guests, though some exclusive workshops may be members-only. Guest policies are clearly stated in each event's description. Pro and Team members often get additional guest privileges.
